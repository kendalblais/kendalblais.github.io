Random Facts About Circles

  • A straight line is a circle with infinite area.
  • Circles have infinite lines of symmetry.
  • Army ants separated from the main foraging party will walk in a continuous circle indefinitely until they die.
  • Technically, the "circle" is the edge of the shape. The entire shape is called a "disk".
  • Humans have no sense of direction. When lost, if there are no known landmarks, we will walk in large circles.
  • There is an annual celebration, Pi Day, that recognizes the constant used in calculating various properties of circles.
