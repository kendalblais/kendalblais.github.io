Circles are Everywhere!

Crop Circles

Not to be confused with the alien conspiracy crop circles, these are caused by center-pivot irrigation where large machines irrigate large areas of land by rotating around a pivot point. From above, they form large circles.

Great Blue Hole

This is a sink hole that formed off the coast of Belize. Its 318m across and 124m deep.

Port Holes

These are window like structures on the sides of ships and submarines. Their purpose is to let light in to the vessel and provide a means to look outside. They are circular because the outsides of these vessels are put under a lot of stress. Building them circular provided a means to evenly distribute the forces so that there were no corners that could crack or break under large pressure.

The wheel

The wheel has been around for a very long time and it has evolved many times. It revolutionized the transportation industry as a result of its circular shape.
